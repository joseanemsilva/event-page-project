About The Festival

Come and discover the magic of forró, the Brazilian northeastern rhythm that has been spreading throughout the world!

Forró Fest, a festa do interior, is a great opportunity to experience the most vibrant festivities from Brazil and celebrate Junine party, the biggest popular culture from Northeast. Forró is a dance, but it is also music and party, and it has the power to involve everyone in one rhythm. Here you will have a complete experience to improve your forró movements, meet other forró lovers, eat traditional food and dance a lot.

Our main purpose is to offer an unforgettable and immersive cultural experience to all participants. In this second year, we will have forró workshops with the greatest forró teachers coming from Brazil and Europe. You will have the opportunity to learn new forró movements and develop even more your dance techniques. To make this experience even more unforgettable, at night you will feel the beat with the most fascinating forró musicians and dance all night.

For more information, email Josy Silva.
